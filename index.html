Dana Hauprich

A senior at Towson University in the BFA track for Photo Imaging. Strange Body is a series of photographs with the intent to blur the lines between object and body. I work primarily in fine art portraiture, often with focus on color comparisons between objects and skin.


Neave Interactive

Neave is a website which allows the audience to scroll through a series of different interactive internet art webpages.



What I found particularly interesting about this website, beyond the fun, procrastinator-friendly interactive elements, was the arrows on each side of the banner. It made the site feel continuous; even though the viewer is scrolling through different websites, the essence of the neave homepage remains intact.
